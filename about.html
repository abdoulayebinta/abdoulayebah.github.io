About Me

I want to build things that make a difference.

After my study in France. I began with programming to create websites for my personal projects but after a while people started to contact me to develop their websites.

I worked with technologies such as ReactJS, Electron, JavaScript, NodeJS, Python, Java and many other languages.

I built a lots of applications for very small companies and organizations like eHealth Africa and many others.

But besides developing, I like to interact with people by teaching what I love.

I've always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns (which I never regret) - from junior software developer, to a data analyst, to a software developer, to a business analyst and finally to a product manager and an entrepreneur. I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems.

As a fullstack web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things, and my passion-driven work ethic to literally change the world. That's why I'm excited to make a big impact at a high growth company.
